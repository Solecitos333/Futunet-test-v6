## 2026-07-06 - Add aria-labels to icon-only quantity buttons\n**Learning:** Icon-only buttons used for cart and product quantity controls (e.g., '-', '+') generated via template literals are missing `aria-label` attributes, making them inaccessible to screen readers.\n**Action:** Add `aria-label="Disminuir cantidad"` and `aria-label="Aumentar cantidad"` to all minus/plus buttons in cart and catalog generation code.
